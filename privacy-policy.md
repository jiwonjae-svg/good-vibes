# Privacy Policy — Daily Glow

**Last Updated: March 18, 2026**

---

Daily Glow ("we", "our", or "us") operates the Daily Glow mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding that information.

By using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

---

## 1. Information We Collect

### Information You Provide Directly
- **Account Information**: When you sign in with Google, we receive your name, email address, and profile photo from Google.
- **Profile Data**: Nickname you set within the App.
- **User Content**: Quotes you submit to the community feed, bookmarks you save, and activity logs (streak check-ins).

### Information Collected Automatically
- **Usage Data**: Pages visited, features used, time spent in the App, session frequency. Collected via Firebase Analytics.
- **Device Information**: Device model, operating system version, app version, language settings.
- **Crash Reports**: Stack traces and error information when the App crashes. Collected via Sentry.

### Information Collected by Third-Party Services
- **Google AdMob**: May collect device advertising identifiers and usage data to serve personalized or non-personalized ads. Subject to Google's Privacy Policy.
- **RevenueCat**: Processes subscription purchases. May collect purchase history and device identifiers. Subject to RevenueCat's Privacy Policy.
- **Firebase (Google)**: Hosts our database (Firestore), authentication, and analytics. Subject to Google's Privacy Policy.

---

## 2. How We Use Your Information

| Purpose | Data Used |
|---------|-----------|
| Provide the App and its features | Account info, profile data, bookmarks, streak data |
| Personalize your experience | Usage data, language preferences |
| Display advertisements | Device advertising ID (AdMob) |
| Process subscription payments | Purchase data (RevenueCat) |
| Monitor and fix crashes | Crash reports (Sentry) |
| Analyze usage to improve the App | Aggregated analytics (Firebase Analytics) |
| Enable community features | Username, community posts |
| Send push notifications | Device push token (if permission granted) |

We do **not** sell your personal information to third parties.

---

## 3. Data Storage and Security

- User data is stored in Google Firebase (Firestore), located on Google Cloud servers.
- Data is transmitted over HTTPS with standard TLS encryption.
- We restrict access to your personal information to employees and services who need it to operate the App.
- We retain your data as long as your account is active or as needed to provide the service.

---

## 4. Data Sharing

We share data only in the following circumstances:

- **Service Providers**: Google (Firebase, AdMob), RevenueCat, Sentry — as described above.
- **Legal Requirements**: If required by law, court order, or government authority.
- **Business Transfer**: In the event of a merger or acquisition, your data may be transferred.
- **Community Content**: Quotes and text you post to the community feed are visible to other users.

## 5. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights:

- **Access**: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
- **Correction**: Request correction of inaccurate data.
- **Deletion**: Request deletion of your account and associated data. You can delete your account from within the App (Settings → Delete Account). We will erase your data within 30 days.
- **Opt-out of personalized ads**: You can opt out of personalized advertising through your device settings (Android: Settings → Privacy → Ads).
- **Withdraw consent**: You may revoke Google Sign-In access at any time through your Google account settings.

To exercise your rights, contact us at: **[dailyglow.support@gmail.com]**
